## D1 — Creator Program & Affiliate Design

**Update 2026-05-07: D1 enriched via web research.**

- **Commission % to creators:** unknown specific % — likely 15–25% range per industry norm. NeuroGum's program terms restrict bidding on trademarked terms but don't publish % rates publicly [T4 vendor — neurogum.com/pages/affiliate-program-terms-and-conditions].
- **Payout structure / tiers:** **"Discord-tier creator guild with GMV thresholds + luxury rewards + founder engagement"** — explicitly described by an industry analyst as "a creator guild, not a transactional program" [T4 — 2xecommerce subscribe.2xecommerce.com]. This is the most distinctive program-architecture finding in the dataset.
- **Sample policy:** Free seeding at very high volume; product is small/cheap to ship (gum/mints), which is a structural advantage over capsule/bottle SKUs for sample velocity.
- **Creator filtering:** Broad. Includes mid-tier UGC creators, podcast personalities, and credentialed-creator stitches.
- **Gamification:** unknown — searched, not found.
- **Creator scale:** **Thousands of affiliates** [T4 Stormy AI].
- **Spark Ad / whitelisting strategy:** **Yes — explicit and core to the playbook.** Spark Ads run on top-performing organic posts. Spark Ads on credentialed creators (RDs, scientists) lend instant credibility on profile titles.
- **Compliance / brief structure:** Repurposes high-authority _external_ content. Brief is essentially "clip a Forbes / Joe Rogan / podcast moment, link to TikTok Shop." This sidesteps creator-generated claim risk because the claim is being made by a third-party authority.
- **Notable creator partnerships:** **Joe Rogan organic mentions are the single most cited brand-build moment.** A Rogan mention had **10× the business impact of Shark Tank** (founder claim, [T2] Inc.). Rogan has mentioned Neuro 10+ times. **2024 brand awareness: 13% national** ("one in seven Americans recognize the brand") almost entirely from TikTok [T4 2xecommerce]. Notable single-creator outcome: **@Espindeezy reportedly drove $450K in sales in 1 month** selling NeuroGum [T4 — TikTok shophousemedia]. Investors include **Scooter Braun, Gary Vaynerchuk, and Steve Aoki** (also a brand partner).
- **What's distinctive:** _Synthesis — Neuro inverted the standard creator-content equation. Most brands brief a creator to make new content. Neuro briefs creators to **clip existing high-authority content** (Rogan, Forbes, Huberman-adjacent voices). The brand's content engine is essentially a clip-licensing-and-redistribution operation. Cheaper, faster, more credible per dollar than UGC-from-scratch._

## D2 — Site & App Teardown

- **PDP structure:** Modern Shopify-style PDP with subscription default; press-mention scroll bar prominent (Forbes, Rogan, etc.).
- **Hero claim language:** "Energy + focus, no jitters / no crash" — outcome-based rather than mechanism-based, easy creator parroting.
- **Quiz funnel:** unknown — pending fresh capture.
- **Bundle structure:** Multi-pack pricing (e.g., 3-pack discount on gum) is the dominant SKU page structure.
- **Free shipping threshold:** unknown.
- **Subscribe-and-save discount:** Reportedly ~20% (industry-norm range) — verify.
- **Checkout type:** Standard Shopify with Shop Pay.
- **Post-purchase upsell:** unknown.
- **Mobile app:** No proprietary app.
- **Trust signals:** Heavy press-bar (Rogan, Forbes, etc.); product is also visible at retail (Target, GNC) which acts as an indirect trust badge.
- **What's distinctive:** _Synthesis — The site doesn't have to do education work because the inbound traffic is already pre-sold by the creator content. The site exists to convert. This is the same pattern as Nello and explains why a quiz funnel may not be the right move for a TikTok-Shop-native traffic mix._

## D4 — Habit-Change Tactics

- **Packaging cues:** Gum tins / mint tins — pocket-sized format. Designed to be carried + visible (desk, gym bag, car). This is a deliberate placement-cue play.
- **Dosage cadence:** As-needed (gum) — much lighter habit-formation friction than daily-pill SKUs because the user doesn't have to "remember to take their nootropic" — they consume it situationally.
- **Onboarding email sequence:** unknown — pending fresh capture.
- **SMS / wallet pass strategy:** unknown.
- **Identity-framing language:** "Limitless"-adjacent positioning; ladders into "high performer" identity. Less explicit Atomic-Habits framing than some peers.
- **Community / lifecycle:** No prominent community surface.
- **Streak / gamification:** N/A — the as-needed format makes streaks irrelevant; this is a different habit primitive than daily-dose supplements.
- **App role in habit:** N/A.
- **What's distinctive:** _Synthesis — Neuro avoids the "perceived optionality" trap that plagues daily-dose supplements (you can skip a magnesium gummy with no consequence). Their format is **situationally indispensable** — you reach for it when you need to focus, the way you'd reach for coffee. This is a structurally different habit category and the most underappreciated lesson of the brand._

## What Rootlab should steal from Neuro

1. **Clip-mining as a creator content engine.** Brief creators to clip existing high-authority content (Huberman on magnesium glycinate, Andy Galpin on ashwagandha, Peter Attia adjacent content) into 15–30s TikTok Shop videos. This is the single highest-leverage creator activity in 2026 and Rootlab is not currently doing it at scale.
2. **Spark Ads on credentialed creator stitches as the paid layer.** Particularly on RDs, MDs, and trainers correcting bad-advice videos. This is what's already working for Nello and Neuro and what Rootlab should be running across all three SKUs.
3. **Press-bar PDP treatment.** A "As mentioned on Huberman Lab / Forbes / etc." scroll bar on PDPs is conversion table-stakes for this category. If Rootlab doesn't have this yet, it's a 1-day fix.

## What Rootlab should NOT copy

- **The format isn't transferable.** Gum/mints are a fundamentally different habit category from daily gummies. Don't try to make MagAshwa "as-needed" — its mechanism (cortisol/sleep over time) requires the daily-ritual frame, not the situational one.
- **Joe Rogan dependency.** Neuro's biggest single growth driver is one podcast host's organic mentions. That's not a strategy you can replicate — it's luck plus persistence. Don't bet your plan on it.
