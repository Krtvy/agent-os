- **Payout structure / tiers:** **Shopify Collabs** is the payout system [T4 vendor]. Performance-tier escalation per ambassador.

- **Creator scale:** **"Thousands of affiliates creating thousands of pieces of content daily"** [T2 WWD; T4 vendor].
- **Spark Ad / whitelisting strategy:** unknown — likely yes given organic-first model.
- **Compliance / brief structure:** unknown — cortisol claims remain FTC-sensitive; investigate next snapshot.
- **Notable creator partnerships:** Per LinkedIn (Michael Rama, Mar 2025), **"Nello Supercalm was the #1 selling product on all of TikTok"** at one moment in early 2025.
- **What's distinctive:** _Synthesis — Nello's combination of **(a) Shopify Collabs payouts + (b) closed Facebook community + (c) monthly competitions + (d) physical meet-ups** is a creator-retention infrastructure most peers under-build. The cash commission is one part of it; the community is the moat. Rootlab should clone this layer specifically — not just the volume seeding but the closed-community-for-top-performers architecture._

## What Rootlab should steal from Nello

1. **Volume creator program at industrial scale.** The 5,600% YoY view jump correlates directly with "thousands of affiliates posting daily." Rootlab's affiliate operation needs to operate at the same order of magnitude — 500–1,000+ active monthly seeders, not a hand-curated program of 50.
2. **Single-serve / travel-format SKU extension.** A MagAshwa travel-sachet line removes the empty-bottle ritual break and unlocks the "perimenopause traveler" niche where Nello's stick format already wins.
3. **TikTok-first → retail halo.** Nello rode TikTok proof-of-concept directly into Target. SNAP did the same with Vitamin Shoppe. Rootlab should set retail launch as a 12–18 month milestone where the TikTok numbers are the only pitch deck.

## What Rootlab should NOT copy

- **Don't compete head-on on cortisol-daytime-stress positioning.** Nello owns that vocabulary. Rootlab's edge with MagAshwa is the _nighttime_ angle (cortisol → sleep) where the magnesium pairing is mechanistically distinct.
- **Don't go drink-mix.** It's a real product/format choice, but gummy is Rootlab's existing strength — switching formats would forfeit the bite-and-treat habit primitive.
