## Standout tactic worth stealing

**Podcast-host-as-channel + free-trial sample funnel.** Magic Mind's growth engine is host-read podcast ads (Tim Ferriss-coded shows, Huberman-adjacent) paired with a "first 7 days at 60% off" trial offer. The free-trial-via-podcast play is the cleanest example of the AG1 podcast playbook applied to a single-serve format — and could be a useful template for Rootlab if/when podcast spend becomes a channel.

## Four-dimension quick read

- **D1 (creator program):** Less TikTok-creator-volume than Tier-A peers; heavier on podcast-host endorsements. Affiliate program exists but is not the primary growth engine.
- **D2 (site/app):** Subscription-default PDP; aggressive trial-offer landing pages targeting podcast-listener traffic; quiz funnel for sleep/focus split.
- **D3 (deals):** Heavy first-trial discounting (60%+ off first 15 days is a recurring offer); standard subscribe-and-save thereafter. Trial-to-sub conversion economics drive the whole funnel.
- **D4 (habits):** Single-serve bottle format — same ritual primitive as Nello (no measurement, binary done/not-done). Sleep variant explicitly anchors to bedtime ritual.

## What Rootlab should steal

1. **Single-serve Sleep / Focus shot SKU** as a TikTok-Shop-friendly impulse format. Travel-friendly. Rootlab's MagAshwa as a pre-mixed "calm shot" would extend the brand into a different occasion (post-work wind-down) without cannibalizing the gummy.
2. **Trial-pricing + auto-convert-to-sub funnel.** First 15 days at 60% off → auto-converts to monthly sub. Higher first-purchase friction-removal than the $9.99 sample funnel from TikAdTools, slightly different shape.
