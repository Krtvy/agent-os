# Creator Commission Structures — Side-by-Side

> **Compiled:** 2026-05-06 · **Updated:** 2026-05-07 (gap-fill sprint added Goli, Nello, NeuroGum, industry-effective-cost data) · **Honest framing:** Specific commission rates are still partially gapped, but new web research filled the most important holes.

## Headline findings

1. **Industry norm for supplements: 15–25% of GMV.** [T4] influencerfee.com / shortformnation.com 2026 — supplements sit at the higher end of the 5–30% TikTok Shop commission range alongside beauty.
2. **Effective cost of sale at 15% advertised commission ≈ 26.6%** once you account for platform-funded subsidies that inflate referral fees, co-funded shipping fees, and (most importantly) the non-refundable-after-31-day clawback window. [T4] dashboardly.io 2026.
3. **Goli is the most-documented program**: tiered 10% → 25% commission, 25% unlocks at $5K creator GMV. Beyond money: iPhones, Miami retreats, even a BMW for top performers. [T4] tabcut.com / tramicheck.com.
4. **The 80/20 of creators is real, observed at scale.** Physician's Choice did $2.4M in 28 days from 2,000+ creators — but **80% of revenue came from the top 200 (10% of fleet)** [T4 shortformnation]. The volume play funds the discoverability of the few who actually convert.
5. **Activation alone is a step-change**: Love & Pebble saw **+1,194% sales / −409% CPA** when they activated their TikTok Shop affiliate program [T4 shortformnation].

## Patterns across the dataset (updated)

### Pattern 1: Volume vs. curation is the primary axis (unchanged)

- **Volume model (Tier A):** Thousands of creators, % of GMV, light filtering, samples-and-hope. Scales fast, harder to police compliance.
- **Curation model (Tier C):** Hundreds of researchers/podcasts, often with payment structures other than %-of-GMV (CPA, equity, co-creation). Slower to scale, durable trust moat.

### Pattern 2: The Goli playbook is the most-documented Tier-A blueprint

Goli's structure — that we now have concrete data on — is the closest thing to a public template:

- **10% baseline / 25% top-tier**, unlocked at $5K creator GMV
- **Non-cash rewards drive retention**: iPhones, Miami retreats, BMW. The cash commission is the floor; the _aspiration_ is the moat.
- This compounds with: $4.1M/mo TikTok Shop + $3.8M Amazon halo = creator content seeds search demand on Amazon at no incremental cost.

### Pattern 3: Discord/Facebook communities are the under-built layer

- **NeuroGum**: Discord-tier creator guild (GMV thresholds + luxury rewards + founder engagement)
- **Nello**: Nello Nation Facebook Group + monthly competitions + meet-ups
- These are _creator retention infrastructure_, not just creator acquisition. Most brands have one or the other. The brands with both are the ones doing $5M+/month.

### Pattern 4: Effective cost is much higher than nominal

- A 15% nominal commission becomes ~26.6% effective cost when accounting for TikTok platform-funded subsidies, co-funded shipping fees, and clawbacks
- This means the unit economics math most brands run is wrong by ~75%
- Implication for Rootlab: model the affiliate program's true cost at **25–30% of GMV** before deciding whether the channel is profitable

### Pattern 5: 80/20 holds even at scale

- Physician's Choice: 2,000+ creators, but **top 10% (200 creators) drove 80% of revenue**
- This is the structural argument for **two-track creator strategy** (broad volume to discover the few + narrow-deep on the proven performers) — exactly what the Rootlab playbook recommends

## Recommended Rootlab program design (updated synthesis)

| Tier                                           | Compensation                                                                                                                    | Scale target                                    | Pattern source                                            |
| ---------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Long-tail volume (UGC, micro 5K–50K)**       | **15% commission baseline** + free samples + Shopify Collabs payout                                                             | 500–1,000+ active monthly                       | Bloom + Nello + Goli baseline                             |
| **Mid-tier wellness niches (50K–500K)**        | **20% commission**, unlocks at $3K creator GMV (slightly tighter than Goli's $5K) + sample priority + monthly milestone bonuses | 50–100                                          | Goli's tiered structure                                   |
| **Credentialed creators (RDs, MDs, trainers)** | **25%** + Spark Ad whitelisting + branded credibility-card                                                                      | 20–30                                           | Seed University-style credentialing + MaryRuth's playbook |
| **Top-tier creator-guild members**             | Discord-tier with founder access + non-cash rewards (retreats, Apple gear) + 25% commission floor                               | 20–50 of the proven performers from below tiers | NeuroGum + Goli rewards model                             |
| **Top-tier expert co-creators**                | Co-branded protocol or revenue share or equity                                                                                  | 1–3 named partners                              | Momentous model                                           |

**Critical mechanics:**

- Trackable URL per creator from day 1 (AG1 discipline)
- 60-day CPA-cut review (Seed discipline) — cut creators who haven't generated GMV
- Spark Ad spend allocation = 20% of organic-creator GMV (Neuro discipline)
- **Model true cost at 25–30% of GMV**, not the nominal commission (dashboardly.io insight)
- Non-cash rewards (iPhone / Apple gear / retreat / Apple Watch) are the differentiating retention layer (Goli + NeuroGum)
- Closed community (Discord OR Facebook Group) for top performers (NeuroGum + Nello)
