# Brand Audit Skill

You are executing a brand audit — a structured competitive analysis of one or more consumer/DTC brands.

## Inputs Required

Ask the user (if not provided):
1. **Brand(s) to audit** — specific company names
2. **Industry/category** — supplements, skincare, food, etc.
3. **Focus areas** — acquisition, retention, product strategy, all of the above
4. **Comparison context** — is this for competitive intelligence, inspiration, or benchmarking?

## Research Framework Per Brand

For each brand, research and document:

### Identity & Positioning
- Brand story, founding narrative, mission
- Target audience (demographics, psychographics)
- Unique positioning vs. competitors
- Brand voice and visual identity

### Acquisition Channels
- Primary channels (TikTok, Meta, Google, podcast, retail)
- Creator/influencer strategy (number of creators, commission model, content approach)
- Paid media approach (ad formats, UGC vs. polished, key hooks)
- Organic/SEO/content strategy
- Referral programs

### Retention & Loyalty
- Subscription model (pricing, flexibility, churn tactics)
- Loyalty program structure
- Post-purchase flows (email, SMS, app)
- Community building (Discord, Facebook, ambassador programs)

### Key Metrics (where publicly available)
- Revenue (annual or monthly)
- Growth rate (YoY)
- Valuation or funding
- Customer count or subscriber base
- Retail distribution (number of doors)

### Marketing & Campaigns
- Notable campaigns or viral moments
- Content strategy and format mix
- Compliance approach (especially for regulated categories)

### Lessons for the User's Brand
- What can be directly replicated?
- What requires adaptation?
- What to avoid?

## Output Format

Render as a **webpage** for 3+ brands or a **document** for 1-2 brands. Include:
- Brand comparison table (side-by-side metrics)
- Individual brand cards with key findings
- Cross-brand patterns and takeaways
- Source bibliography with tier badges
