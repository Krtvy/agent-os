## Regulatory Notes

- FDA: supplements regulated under DSHEA (1994). Structure/function claims OK, disease claims require pre-approval.
- FTC: advertising must be truthful, not misleading. Substantiation required.
- TikTok compliance: No medical condition mentions, no "cure/treat/prevent" language
- Safe language: supports, maintains, promotes, helps with, encourages
- Banned: cure, treat, prevent, fix, eliminate; medical conditions; extreme before/after
